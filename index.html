Dutch Beatbox Battle is more than just the Dutch Beatbox championships.

The Dutch championships are organized yearly in a city in The Netherlands. Momentarily hosted by HurraKane Tha SoundZtorm (Dutch Champion 2013) in Rotterdam, in association with Boom Snap Clap and RSCW. Besides the event, DBB aims to be a platform for beatboxers in The Netherlands.

Dutch Beatbox Academy

Are you living in The Netherlands and do you want to learn the art of human beatbox? Sign up now for Dutch Beatbox Academy, as we begin our first lessons next month.

The winner will represent The Netherlands during the World Championships

Dutch Beatbox Battle has been providing champions for the World Championships since the beginning of the event. Our own B-Art became the vice champion of the world and Monkie reached the quarter finals.

We're heading towards Dutch Beatbox Battle 2026

After some time without a Dutch Beatbox Battle, we're working hard to realize the next Championship event: Dutch Beatbox Battle 2026. Before that time we have some jams and other events planned: so keep following us for more information.
